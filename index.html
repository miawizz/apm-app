Playful Strategies

Tap a strategy for a reminder and the example video

What it is: Bringing inanimate objects to life — like a toy, snack, or sock — to redirect energy with silliness.

  • Toothbrush "cleaning ladies" begging not to work
  • Broccoli yelling "Just a trim!"
  • Shoes that want to dance

What it is: Pretending to be a robot, machine, or voice assistant.

  • Rewind button for do-overs
  • GPS voice giving instructions
  • "Volume knob" for behavior

What it is: Acting shocked or upset when your child does exactly what you want.

  • "Don’t eat that broccoli while I’m not looking!"
  • "If you take that medicine, I’ll moo like a cow!"
  • "Nooo, don’t smile at me!"

What it is: Turning tasks into a game, challenge, or silly contest.

  • Race against the clean-up song
  • Who can blow the biggest bubble?
  • Silent screaming challenge

What it is: Pretending something magical or mysterious is happening.

  • Freeze spells or floating objects
  • Lotion disappears with a gasp
  • Force field until they say "please"

What it is: Turning routines into musical numbers, chants, or dances.

  • Opera singing instead of yelling
  • Silent disco transitions
  • Toothbrushing jingles

What it is: Taking on a silly persona or letting them do it instead of just giving directions.

  • Butt-Wiper 3000
  • Villain trying to vacuum up toys
  • Announcer at the “bedtime Olympics”

What it is: Narrating real life like an adventure or telling silly made-up stories.

  • The panda mom who roared too loud
  • Fortunately/unfortunately games
  • Swamp alligator with stinky breath

What it is: Using dramatic, poetic, or VIP language to frame boring stuff as fun or fancy.

  • “Welcome to the bedtime spa”
  • “We’re entering the grocery gauntlet!”
  • “Sir Broccoli must save the castle!”

What it is: Regulating through movement, breathing, or silly physical contact.

  • Power-on shoulders: scrunch/drop
  • Blow each other across the room
  • Lemon face / orange face

What it is: Gibberish, silly voices, fart monsters — for interrupting tension and reconnecting.

  • Banana phone conversation
  • 1, 2, 3… FART! instead of "cheese"
  • “What have you DONE?!” in goofy voice
